Top Places to Visit in Seoul

BY YUKI SOO
PHOTO PROVIDED BY UNSPLASH WEBSITE
AUG 30,2021

South Korea's capital and largest city is Seoul. This place is also one of the most visited city in South Korea by travelers. Seoul has not only modern buildings, but also some traditional buildings, such as hanoks (traditional Korean houses), traditional markets, and Korean castles. So, this place is one of the best place to visit in Korea as they not only show you their latest trend but also their tradition and culture. There are three places that I wanted to share with you which is Gwangjang Night Market, Myeongdong and Insadong.

Gwangjang Market

One of the earliest and largest traditional markets in South Korea is Gwangjang Market. Gwangjang Market consists of many different types of stores such as fashion, daily need, food etc. Street food is popular in this market. They have Spicy Rice Cake, Kimbap, Korean pancake, Dumplings, Donuts and many more. The most popular food is Bindaetteok (Korean Mung Bean pancake), Spicy Rice cake, Korean traditional Donut and Nude Kimbap. The owner are very kind and friendly so I recommend you to visit Gwangjang Market if you are interested with their traditions and food culture.

Address

Location: 03195 88 Changgyeonggung-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

Hours of Operations

Daily, 09:00 - 18:00 *Closed Sundays*
Food Alley, Daily 09:00 - 23:00 *Closed Sundays*
Guide Shopping Street, Daily 10:00 - 19:00 *Closed Sundays*

Myeongdong

Myeongdong is a place full of cosmetic shops, luxury brands, cafes and restaurants. Myeongdong are mostly visited by youngsters as it is a place that follows the latest trend in Korea. So, if you want something that is from the latest trend Myeongdong is a perfect place. While you shop for goods, you can have some street food at the Myeongdong Night Market. Some famous foods are grilled cheese lobster, Korean egg buns and tornado potatoes. There are rows of restaurants in Myeongdong, selling different types of food. You are able to find the most famous franchaise brand in Myeongdong. For example, fried chicken franchaise such as BHC, Kyochon, Oppadak and Mom's Touch.

Insadong

Insadong is a popular place for its cafes and traditional tea houses. Insadong main street is connected to different alley that was fill with shops and antique sellers. The alley ambience is a mixture of mordern and tradition times of Korea. Most of the shops are built by hanoks (traditional Korean houses)This place is popular for photo taking too. For Korean traditional tea house, I recommend 47th Avenue Tea House. They sell Korean traditional desserts such as Patbingsu (Korean Red bean shaved ice), Cinnamon Ginger Punch and Korean Rice cake. As for Instagram worthy cafe, I recommend Mil Toast House. Their most popular menu is steamed bun.

Top Places to Visit in Jeju

BY YUKI SOO
PHOTO PROVIDED BY UNSPLASH WEBSITE
AUG 30,2021

Among Korea's islands, Jeju is the most visited. Jeju is famous for its rural culture and natural resources. Jeju Island is surrounded by the sea so, they are famous for their seafood. If you wanted to visit Jeju make sure you try to rent a car as hotel and restaurants in Jeju are quite far from each other.

Dongmun Market

Dongmun Market is the largest traditional market in Jeju Island. Through there you would found some of their local produce and Jeju specialty. Jeju is famous for their tangerine and seafood and you can buy it at the market. Dongmun Market also consists of a fish market where locals sells their catch of the day. If you want to have some fresh sashimi you can buy the seafood you prefer and ask them to prepare it for you. Other than that, the market also sells street foods. You can try out some Jeju specialty such as omeji tteok (Korean rice cake that was coated with red bean) and jeju madarine Bungeoppang (madarine jam pancake bread).

Address

Location: 20, Gwuandeok-ro 14-gil, Jeju

Hours of Operations

Every day 08:00-21:00

Osullac Tea Museum

Jeju is also famous for it's organically grown, green tea. Jeju green tea is grown on the Jeju green tea farm, with plenty of sunshine and water. Koreans use green tea in many different ways, such as in skin care products and food. So, I recommend you check out Osullac Tea Museum if you are a tea lover. You can visit their cafe for their green tea desserts such as green tea ice cream and green tea cake. This is a also a perfect place to buy souvenir for your family.

Address

Location: 15 Sinhwayeoksa-ro, Andeok-myeon, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do, South Korea

Hours of Operations

09:00 – 18:00, open all year around

Udo Island

Udo Island is a small island on Jeju and it is 3.5 kilometers away from Jeju. You can have a walk and enjoy the scenery of the island. Udo Island is famous for their peanut and you can try their peanut ice cream. The peanut ice cream is vanilla ice cream with roasted peanut toppings. The amazing thing is you can have it by the sea and take some wonderful photo. Moreover, you should try the seafood ramen at their restaurant. The seafood is fresh and the ramen broth is super delicious.
