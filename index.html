About Us

We will share the beauty of Korea through their food. The cuisine of Korea comprises the culinary arts and customs of its people. We like the taste and meaning behind every kind of Korean food. We will also share some of our own Korean inspired recipes with you. Through this, you will be able to learn more about Korean food and its culture. This magazine will also show you some of the hottest food trends happening in Korea and some of the places you must visit in Korea.

Top Places in Korea

South Korea is a beautiful country with a long history. Koreans are proud of their country and culture. They have many beautiful and meaningful heritages that still existed long ago. Heritage, traditions and culture are well preserved by their people. We will show you some of the top delicacies that you must visit in Korea. Different regions of Korea are famous for different types of food because of their different local products.

Healthy and Low Calories Recipes

We will show you some of our favorite recipes that can be made easily with minimal ingredients. It does not require any additional cooking utensils. You just need a non-stick fry pan and a turner. The recipes are easy to make even if you are an amateur. All of the ingredients listed are easy to find in your neighborhood supermarket.
