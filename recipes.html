Korean Inspire Recipes

Korean Rose Tteokbokki

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang
  • 4 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoons of sugar
  • 200 ml of milk
  • one slice of cheese

Directions

  1. First boil the rice cake in boiled water for about 2 minutes.
  2. After two minutes, remove the rice cake from the water and set it aside.
  3. Add in milk and the all other ingredients except cheese into a saucepan.
  4. Wait till it boiled.
  5. After boiled add in any ingredients you like into the saucepan together with the rice cake and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add in the cheese.
  7. Stir well and done.

Korean Army Stew

Ingredients (Soup)

  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang
  • 4 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 cube of anchovy stock
  • 1 litre of water

Directions

  1. Add in any ingredients you like into the pot such ham, sausages, white cabbage, mushrooms and etc.
  2. Add in the ingredients os the soup base into the pot.
  3. When the ingredients is cooked, you can served it.

Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoons of gochujang
  • 3 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 500g of smocked bacon
  • 1 chopped onion

Directions

  1. First, stir fry the chopped onion until golden brown with medium heat.
  2. Next, add in the smocked bacon.
  3. Add in all of the ingredients into the frying pan.
  4. Cook it for about 10 minutes and you are done.

Healthy and Low Calories Recipes

Low Calorie Tofu Sushi

Ingredients

  • Tofu sushi skin
  • Tofu
  • Egg

Directions

  1. First, try to remove as many water as you can from the tofu
  2. Next, stir fry the tofu on a pan until most of the water has evaporated.
  3. Beat an egg and make some scramble egg.
  4. Add in tofu and egg into the tofu skin and you are done.
  5. You could add on some topping such as mayo or wasabi.

No Flour Kimchi Pancake

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Tofu
  • Egg

Directions

  1. First, try to remove as many water as you can from the tofu.
  2. Next, stir fry the tofu on a pan until most of the water has evaporated.
  3. Try to shape the tofu into a small circle.
  4. Beat an egg and pour a little bit of egg onto the tofu until it fully covered it.
  5. Top on some kimchi onto the pancake.
  6. Fry it until it turns golden brown. Then you are done.

Morning Egg Mayo Bread

Ingredients

  • Morning bread
  • 3 Egg
  • 4 tablespoons of Mayo
  • 1/2 Teasponns of Salt
  • 1 teasponns of Sugar

Directions

  1. First, boiled some water in a pot that can fully covered the egg when it was put in.
  2. Next, reduce the heat to medium and slowly add in the room temperature egg.
  3. After 7 and a half minutes, remove it from the pot in put it into a pot of cold water.
  4. Peel the egg and smash the egg into small pieces.
  5. Add in mayo,sugar and salt.
  6. Mix it well.
  7. Add it into the morning bread and you are done.
